Instructions:

1. Go to File > Create Avatar, or simply Alt + N. Then enter the information.

2. Go to File > Add Food, or simply Alt + X. Enter name of food (e.g. simply be Teddy's Burger) and the approximate amount of calories.

3. Go to View > View Chart or Alt + B and a frame will pop up with a pie data chart displaing your limit and the amount you intake.
